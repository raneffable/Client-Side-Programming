Simple Guide

Every Character will spawn in Heaven's Village

Leveling

Player can leveling by killing monster outside of Heaven's Village until reach level 105.

Craft Equipment

Player can craft the equipment on furnace inside Heaven's Village.

Finding Material

Player can find the material "Golden Spoon" on Plume City.
Player can find the material "Silver Macaroni" on Tellus City.
Player can find the material "Bronze Wood" on Dragon City.
